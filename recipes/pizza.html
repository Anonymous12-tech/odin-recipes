Pizza

Description

Pizza is a baked flatbread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like vegetables, meats, or herbs.

Pizza is a savory dish of Italian origin featuring a crispy crust loaded with cheese, sauce, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) or as high as it goes.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness
  3. Transfer dough to a baking tray or pizza stone
